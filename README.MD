## üéôÔ∏è How to Use MixSplitR

Using MixSplitR is straightforward. Follow these steps to record and organize your music:

1. **Start a New Recording:**
   Click the "Record" button to begin capturing your mix. Make sure your audio source is set correctly.

2. **Splitting Mixes:**
   Once your recording is complete, MixSplitR will automatically split larger files into smaller tracks based on silence detection and BPM analysis.

3. **Identify Metadata:**
   MixSplitR will scan each track and gather metadata such as song title, artist, and album. This information helps organize your collection.

4. **Organize Your Library:**
   After metadata is added, you can browse and sort your tracks. Use the provided features to create playlists or folders within the app.

5. **Save Your Collection:**
